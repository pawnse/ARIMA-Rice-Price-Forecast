THE FORECAST

February 7, 2022

Dataset from: openstat.psa.gov.ph
Data ranges from January 2012 - November 2021


Abstract

The growing prices of rice have a huge economic impact on us Filipino since it is a staple food to us. The data used is from the Philippine Statistics Authority from January 2012 to November 2021 to make a prediction and forecast the price of well-milled rice on NCR. ARIMA Model was used in this study and determine not just the trends but also the effects of the pandemic on the price.

Chech if the data is stationary

In [671]:
from statsmodels.tsa.stattools import adfuller
    def adfuller_test(df_rice):
        result=adfuller(df_rice)
        labels = ['ADF Test Statistic','p-value','#Lags Used','Number of Observations Used']
        for value,label in zip(result,labels):
            print(label+' : '+str(value) )
        if result[1] <= 0.05:
            print("time series is stationary")
        else:
            print("time series is non-stationary ")

# Setting frequency offset
    week = "Mon Tue Wed Thu Fri Sat"
    nosun = pd.offsets.CustomBusinessDay(weekmask=week)
    
    # Make daterange of forecast dates
    idx = pd.date_range(start = '2021-11-01', end = '2022-11-01', freq = nosun)
    
    # Fit model predictions
    future = model_fit.get_forecast(314)
    
    # Format as dataframe
    future_df = pd.DataFrame(future.predicted_mean)
    future_df['DATE'] = idx
    future_df = future_df.set_index('DATE')
    future_df.columns = ['forecast']

Evaluating the RSME

In [692]:
mse = mean_squared_error(df_rice['NCR'], df_rice['training'])
    rmse = sqrt(mse)
    print('RMSE value: %f' % rmse)
    
RMSE value: 0.861043

Conclusion

The RSME value shows a minimal error and ranging in a good model that is 0.861043 RSME.

The Ljung-Box test accepts the null hypothesis indicating good fit in the model.

The autocorrelation of the residuals show no significant values.

The other parts of the summary shows that the Autoregressive term is significant.

The result suggested that the ARIMA(1,1,0) is a good fit for forecasting a Well-Milled Rice Price in NCR at the year 2012 to the present time.
